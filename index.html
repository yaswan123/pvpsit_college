About PVPSIT

Welcome to PVPSIT

Established in 1997, PVPSIT has been a beacon of education and innovation. Nestled in the heart of Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, our campus spans over 60 acres and offers a vibrant environment for learning and growth.

Our college is known for its exceptional academic programs, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. With a commitment to nurturing creativity and excellence, we offer a wide range of courses in science, technology, arts, and humanities.

We take pride in fostering an inclusive and supportive community, where students are encouraged to achieve their full potential. Join us on a journey of discovery, innovation, and success at PVPSIT.
